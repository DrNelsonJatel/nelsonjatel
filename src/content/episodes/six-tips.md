Every network study lives or dies on decisions you make before you open the
software. Who counts as a node. What counts as a tie. What you are willing to
claim when the graph looks tidy. These six tips are the ones I find myself
repeating most often.

None of them are about software. They are about the judgement that turns a
pretty picture into a finding you can defend in a room full of people who would
rather you were wrong.
